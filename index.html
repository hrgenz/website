Programs and Services

The Hampton Roads Gen-Z Commission provides practical opportunities for young adults to contribute to the region through leadership development, civic education, and service.

Leadership Development

Workshops, panel discussions, and networking sessions focused on professional growth and public leadership.

Civic Engagement

Programs that connect young people with local decision-makers, policy conversations, and public service pathways.

Community Partnerships

Collaborations with schools, nonprofits, museums, and employers to expand opportunities for regional youth.

Volunteer and Mentorship

Structure ways for members and supporters to give time, skills, and mentorship to the next generation of leaders.

Community Impact Priorities

Our organization focuses on outcomes that strengthen Hampton Roads and reduce youth outmigration by improving local opportunity.

  • Increase youth participation in civic and community leadership.
  • Connect members to internships, mentorship, and career pathways.
  • Support local organizations through volunteer projects and partnerships.
  • Provide events where young adults can engage directly with regional leaders.

Our Mission

We empower Generation Z to lead, serve, and build long-term opportunity in Hampton Roads.

Inspire, inform, involve, and invest in young leaders as they help shape Hampton Roads.

Inspire

Help young adults see a future in Hampton Roads and take action in their neighborhoods and workplaces.

Inform

Provide clear information on regional issues, civic processes, and opportunities for growth.

Involve

Create hands-on opportunities for members to lead projects, collaborate with partners, and serve the community.

Invest

Invest in youth leadership through mentoring, volunteering, and financial support for mission programs.

Get Involved

Membership is free. Join if you want to lead, volunteer, and build a stronger future for Hampton Roads.

Members receive invitations to events, leadership opportunities, and community projects at no cost. Supporters can also contribute through donations and partnership opportunities.
