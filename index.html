Our Mission

Gen-Z has the power to unlock a bright future for Hampton Roads. Our mission is to help them unleash that power.

Inspire, inform, involve, and invest in Gen-Z as they take lead in Hampton Roads.

Inspire

Get Gen-Z excited about their future in Hampton Roads and inspire them to action.

Inform

Keep Gen-Z in the know about regional issues, civic process, and exciting opportunities.

Involve

Offer powerful opportunities for Gen-Z to lead and shape the future of Hampton Roads.

Invest

Support Z-aligned mission in the community through time, money, and mentorship.

Get Involved

Membership in the Z-commish is a pledge to step up and shape the future of Hampton Roads.

Our members get invites to events and leadership opportunities at no cost. But, most importantly, they are part of an authentic community with real impact.
